What is
padel?

Padel is a racket sport originating from Mexico commonly described as a combination of squash and tennis. More recently, padel is one of the fastest growing sports with courts all around Europe. Uniquely, padel is always played in doubles, making it the perfect sport to play with friends of all ages. Padel is easy and simple to learn, no prior experience is needed to pick up the sport and play with confidence because the court is smaller and surrounded by walls that make it easier to retrieve the ball.
